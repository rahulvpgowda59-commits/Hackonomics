About Hackonomics

Hackonomics 2026 is an Ideathon and Innovation Challenge organized by the Money Plant Club of BMS Institute of Technology & Management. The event brings together creative minds to develop innovative solutions across technology and sustainability domains. Participants will brainstorm, design, and present groundbreaking ideas that solve real-world problems using emerging technologies.

Why Participate?

💡 Innovation

Turn your ideas into impactful solutions and showcase your creativity.

🤝 Networking

Connect with innovators, mentors, and industry professionals.

🏆 Recognition

Get recognized by experts and win exciting prizes.

🚀 Learning

Gain experience in problem solving, pitching and teamwork.
